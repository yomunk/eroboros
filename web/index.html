A Modular Synthesizer for Sex. A non representational and abstracted way to co-play with a partner based off of a biometric feedback loop. Information is taken through the user with a wearable arm piece and is transmitted into movement on the other users toy of choice. Both users are transmitting and receiving information in tandem creating a feedback loop of pleasure. This website serves as an interface and an immersive platform with sound/visuals controlled by the data as well. There is a flexible mapping of inputs and outputs that can be modified for personal preferences. Project created at Pittsburgh OOOHack 2024 with Yoyo, Zev, Jude, Jenna, Ava, Bryn, Taylor, and Lex.

Our intentions behind this were to create a way to engage in pleasure that is outside of the regular human experience and ego. It allows an inherent form of privacy through obfuscation and can also be a way to decouple trauma from sex as being present in the same physical space is not necessary. This is a new and exciting way to engage with the body and experience sensations outside of the norm.
